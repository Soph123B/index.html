FAQ
How does the SUI Smash Bros token work?

The token is central to the SUI Smash Bros ecosystem. Players use it to access exclusive games, compete in tournaments for crypto rewards, and purchase or trade NFTs like characters and items.

Where can I buy SUI Smash Bros tokens?

You can purchase tokens directly through our website or on select SUI-supported exchanges. Simply visit our “Where to Buy” section for more details on purchasing options and partner platforms.

What games are available in the SUI Smash Bros ecosystem?

Our games are inspired by old-school arcade classics, revamped with meme culture and internet-inspired characters. From battle arenas to leaderboard-driven competitions, each game offers unique ways to earn and engage.

How do I earn rewards in the SUI Smash Bros games?

Players can earn rewards by participating in games and versing each other, climbing leaderboards, and winning tournaments.

Is there a community aspect to SUI Smash Bros?

Absolutely! We prioritize community engagement, allowing token holders to vote on game updates, new character ideas, and ecosystem features. This ensures that our players have a say in shaping the future of SUI Smash Bros.

What’s the roadmap for SUI Smash Bros?

Our roadmap includes game development, partnerships with other SUI ecosystem projects, and a launch on the SUI handheld device. Each stage aims to add value to our token and increase the player base for sustainable, long-term growth.

Are there opportunities to stake SUI Smash Bros tokens?

We’re exploring staking options that will allow token holders to earn passive income while contributing to the growth of our ecosystem. Stay tuned for updates on our staking program!

How is SUI Smash Bros different from other crypto games?

Unlike many crypto games, SUI Smash Bros brings an element of classic arcade nostalgia mixed with internet culture. It’s a place where retro gamers, meme lovers, and crypto enthusiasts can all come together. Plus, our commitment to community-driven development means the players truly shape our ecosystem.

How does the leaderboard work?

The leaderboard is a core part of our experience, letting players compete for top spots and exclusive rewards.

 

How do I rank on the leaderboard?

 

Your position on the leaderboard is based on the total points you accumulate from gameplay in one 15 minute session.

 

Can I win prizes without being the top player?

 

Yes! Every second or third player on the leaderboard is randomly selected to win a prize, giving everyone a fair shot at rewards even if you aren’t in the top spot.

How does the point system work?

The points system in SUI Smash Bros is designed to keep every match engaging and competitive.

 

How do points work in each game?

 

Players go head-to-head, with each match lasting 15 minutes. Your goal is to score as many points as possible during this time. The player with the highest score at the end of the match wins.

 

Do points from each game carry over to the leaderboard?

 

Yes, points you score in each game are updated as your progress.

UTILITY OF SUI SMASH BROS

Gaming Hub with Nostalgia

SUI Smash Bros is a platform where players can experience their favourite retro games like *Super Mario Bros*, *Mario Kart*, and more. However, each game is given a fresh twist by replacing classic characters with popular meme coins from the SUI chain, turning the experience into a fun, unique, and engaging journey for both gamers and crypto enthusiasts.

 

Earning Mechanism
Players earn rewards by playing the games, competing in tournaments, and ranking high on leaderboards. These rewards come in the form of $SMASH tokens or other exclusive prizes, integrating both gaming and crypto in a seamless ecosystem.

HOW IT WORKS

Free trial
New users can access a free trial for each game, letting them play a limited number of rounds to get a feel for the platform and the games. This allows for a low-entry barrier, drawing in both casual and hardcore gamers without immediate financial commitment.

Paid Access After Trial
Once the free trial ends, users will need to pay a small fee to continue playing. This fee can be paid in $SMASH tokens, increasing the token’s utility. The pay-to-play model keeps the ecosystem sustainable while rewarding active players. Competition & Prize Pools: After users pay to play, a portion of that payment is taken by the $SMASH team to maintain the platform. The rest is pooled into the prize funds for ongoing competitions and tournaments, giving players a chance to win crypto rewards, $SMASH tokens, and exclusive NFTs.

HOW OUR TEAM/COMPANY MAKES MONEY

Platform Fees
The $SMASH team takes a small percentage (e.g., 10-20%) of the fees collected from users who pay to play. This portion goes toward platform maintenance, game development, marketing, and team operations.

 

Partnership Integrations
The company can partner with other SUI ecosystem projects or meme-based tokens to feature their characters in-game, earning additional revenue from licensing or integration fees.

 

NFT Marketplace
As the platform evolves, the team can launch an in-game NFT marketplace where users can buy, sell, and trade meme-inspired assets, characters, and in-game items. A small transaction fee from each trade can also contribute to revenue.

 

Exclusive In-Game Items and Staking
The company can release limited edition in-game items (like special meme skins or power-ups) for a premium, or offer staking features where users lock up $SMASH tokens for passive rewards.

HOW USERS CAN MAKE MONEY

Competing for Rewards
 Users can earn money by competing in games and tournaments. As players climb the leaderboard, they earn $SMASH tokens or crypto prizes, incentivizing regular participation and competition.

 

Leaderboard Prizes
The leaderboard will allow players to earn rewards based on their performance in each round. For example, top players could win a larger percentage of the prize pool, while others could earn random rewards just for being in the top 3-10 spots, ensuring that everyone has a chance to win.

 

Selling NFTs
Players who collect in-game NFTs can sell them on the marketplace for a profit. These NFTs could represent unique characters, items, or even special access to exclusive games or tournaments, providing users with an additional revenue stream.

 

Staking Rewards
If the project introduces staking, users who hold $SMASH tokens can lock their tokens in exchange for additional rewards. This can incentivize holding $SMASH for the long term while contributing to the ecosystem’s stability.
